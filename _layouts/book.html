Book Overview

This book is a mix of thought leadership and instruction. It is the how-to guide for the emerging artisan economy and more. It will outline how today’s creative entrepreneurs can ensure a successful venture as the economy vacillates between recovery and recession, how they can create an honest, unique identity in an era of such rapid innovation and change, how they can stand out in a saturated marketplace when we now have more innovators and makers than ever before, how they can bring together a team when so many workers are joining the gig economy, and how they can bring their vision to life and find confidence in their role as leaders of this new economic moment.
